About Service Human

Service Human, Inc. exists for one purpose: to help people. We do not exist to make a profit, to chase status, or to build an empire. We exist to make it easier for one human being to help another.

Our commitments are simple:

  • People first. We help individuals without conditions or labels.
  • No executive bloat. No sky-high salaries, bonuses, or perks.
  • Transparency. Donors can see how funds are allocated and what has been accomplished.
  • Collaboration. We work with governments and partners, but we are not controlled by them.

If you want a glossy marketing campaign, this probably isn’t the organization for you. If you want your money to quietly go to work for people who need it, you’re in the right place.

How Funds Are Used

Our principle is straightforward: keep overhead light and help heavy.

Category Target Allocation Notes
Direct support to people 80–90% Housing, food, transit, basic needs, emergency support.
Service Human HUB operations 5–15% Safe spaces, staff, supplies, and local partnerships.
Administration & compliance 0–5% Accounting, legal, required filings. No unnecessary layers.

These percentages are targets, not guarantees. We publish periodic summaries so donors can see how actual funds were used over time.

Founder’s Note

By Trevor Marcus • Service Human, Inc.

This morning, while trying to do something as simple as log into a dashboard, everything broke. Every button froze, every screen failed, every system stalled. For a moment, it felt like the universe was saying “not today.”

But here’s what I realized in that moment:

There is no version of reality where I fail.

My default mode has always been the same:

  • If it breaks, I rebuild it stronger.
  • If it’s blocked, I reroute.
  • If it’s taken, I reinvent.
  • If I’m slowed down, I prepare.

That’s not just a motivational phrase — that’s how I’ve survived my life. That’s how I’ve created everything I’ve ever built from nothing. That’s why Service Human exists at all.

And ironically, the more resistance I feel, the more confirmation I get that this mission is necessary.

Because the truth is simple:

If one person can be stopped by a broken login page, a hacked account, a delay, or a barrier, then the system was never built for them.

And the system should be built for them.

Service Human, Inc. was created out of the belief that human dignity should not depend on money, status, or access. It should not depend on whether you can afford the “premium tier.” It should not depend on whether algorithms or corporate structures decide your value.

Our mission is to build a world where:

  • People who fall through the cracks have a place to land.
  • People who were forgotten finally get remembered.
  • People who were blocked their entire life finally get a clear path forward.

And the irony is that today — while everything “failed” — I became even more certain of what I’m building.

I wasn’t angry. I wasn’t panicking. I wasn’t defeated.

I was calm.

Because deep down, I know something most people never learn:

  • If they block me, I’ll build a new path.
  • If they limit me, I’ll expand beyond the limit.
  • If they try to stop the mission, the mission will only evolve.

You cannot stop someone whose purpose is bigger than the obstacle.

Service Human isn’t just a nonprofit. It isn’t a brand. It isn’t a project.

It’s a new model of what humanity can be.

Every roadblock that tries to stand in the way — technical, human, or systemic — only proves the need for what we’re building.

I don’t quit. I don’t break. I don’t give up.

I adapt. I rebuild. I return stronger.

And that is exactly what we will do for the world.

— Trevor Marcus
Founder, Service Human, Inc.
