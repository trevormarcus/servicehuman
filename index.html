About Service Human

Service Human, Inc. exists for one purpose: to help people. We do not exist to make a profit, to chase status, or to build an empire. We exist to make it easier for one human being to help another.

Our commitments are simple:

  • People first. We help individuals without conditions or labels.
  • No executive bloat. No sky-high salaries, bonuses, or perks.
  • Transparency. Donors can see how funds are allocated and what has been accomplished.
  • Collaboration. We work with governments and partners, but we are not controlled by them.

If you want a glossy marketing campaign, this probably isn’t the organization for you. If you want your money to quietly go to work for people who need it, you’re in the right place.

How Funds Are Used

Our principle is straightforward: keep overhead light and help heavy.

Category Target Allocation Notes
Direct support to people 80–90% Housing, food, transit, basic needs, emergency support.
Service Human HUB operations 5–15% Safe spaces, staff, supplies, and local partnerships.
Administration & compliance 0–5% Accounting, legal, required filings. No unnecessary layers.

These percentages are targets, not guarantees. We publish periodic summaries so donors can see how actual funds were used over time.
